Our Story

A Legacy of Building Excellence

Founded in 1998 by Michael Torres, BuildPro Construction began as a small residential contractor with a big vision. With a team of just five dedicated professionals, we took on our first project: a modest family home in upstate New York. That project set the standard for everything we would become.

Over the past 25 years, we have grown into one of the region's most respected construction firms, delivering over 500 projects across commercial, residential, industrial, and infrastructure sectors. Our growth has been guided by a simple principle: do exceptional work, treat people with respect, and never compromise on quality.

Today, BuildPro employs over 150 skilled professionals and operates across the tri-state area. We continue to push the boundaries of what's possible in construction while maintaining the personal touch and commitment to excellence that has defined us from day one.
